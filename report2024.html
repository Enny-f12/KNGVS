2023 Report


KNGVS™, which is in its sixth edition, is a fun-filled, innovative, and unconventional annual hands-on garden-to-fork food system transformation programme and community service project organised by the Department of Human Nutrition and Dietetics University of Ibadan (UI) Nigeria for school-age children and their families during the long vacation of schools in August.

This year, KNGVS™ was tagged ‘Amplified Edition’, themed Grow it, Cook it, Love it! Amplified IMPACT

Nutri - Garden - 2 - Fork Adventure

From 07 August to 01 September, 2023, with support from various sponsors and partners, KNGVS successfully added colour and adventure to children’s summer vacation programme in their respective schools to make their holiday a fun learning experience.
Activities included:
- Hands-on nutri-gardening to grow and harvest four local veggies, in their schools
- Amplified nutri-kitchen held every Wednesday, in collaboration with O-Beth
- Interactive nutrition education lessons tied to gardening and kitchen activities
- Family newsletters for family members to learn along with us in their homes
- Community KNGVS engagement with children in two communities in Ibadan in collaboration with Waste4Skills
- Growit, Cookit, Loveit public exhibition and family fun day.

KNG Corps Volunteers

Our well-trained volunteers at KNGVS make us thrive. From 19 July to 4 August, 2023, the volunteers received intensive training and orientation, which equipped them with knowledge and skills for technical and administrative tasks.
The dedicated efforts of their time and energy throughout August and the first week of September, to facilitate the KNGVS activities resulted in a vibrant, and blissful holiday experience for the Schools and the children.

KNGVS Amplified Nutri - Kitchen

This exciting and highly engaging activity, took place every Wednesday throughout the month of August, in the Department of Human Nutrition and Dietetic kitchen and in collaboration with O’beth treats, Ibadan. Mrs Elizabeth Shotunde, CEO, Master Chef and gardening enthusiast, inspired and brought to life KNGVS ‘Amplified Nutri-kitchen’, This new feature of KNGVS brought together children, youths, adults (parents) to learn hands-on and gain creative skills to prepare simple, healthy and tasty meals with veggies and fruits.

World Food Day KNGVS Event

KNGVS in collaboration with the Association of Nutrition Science Students (ANSS), Dept. of Human Nutrition and Dietetics participated in the global celebrations of the FAO’s World Food Day (October 16, 2023) with the theme: "Water is Life, Water is Food. Leave No One Behind." This theme aimed to spotlight water as the fundamental source of life, sustenance, and food”. From 26 to 30 October, 2023, our team of 25 dedicated volunteers (youths) visited four schools, engaging over 200 pupils and 30 teachers. Using communication tools provided by the FAO, we effectively conveyed the significance of water in sustaining life and food through four educative videos (Water is life, water is food, Water heroes are #FoodHeroes, Eight actions we can all take to save water, WFD official music video), hands-on outdoor gardening, water filtration experiment, WFD poster contest explanation, singing and dancing. The children had fun all he way!
