Amplified Kitchen

KNGVS Amplified Nutri-kitchen

This exciting and highly engaging activity, took place every Wednesday throughout the month of August, in the Department of Human Nutrition and Dietetic kitchen and in collaboration with O’beth treats, Ibadan. Mrs Elizabeth Shotunde, CEO, Master Chef and gardening enthusiast, inspired and brought to life KNGVS ‘Amplified Nutri-kitchen’, This new feature of KNGVS brought together children, youths, adults (parents) to learn hands-on and gain creative skills to prepare simple, healthy and tasty meals with veggies and fruits.

Our Amplified Nutri-recipe Models

Each kitchen session and recipe day saw us creatively using harvested veggies from our Nutri-garden (and other veggies) and food products from Golden Penny Foods, Peak 4-5-6 and Peak choco
