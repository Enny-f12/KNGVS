World Food Day KNGVS Event

KNGVS in collaboration with the Association of Nutrition Science Students (ANSS), Dept. of Human Nutrition and Dietetics participated in the global celebrations of the FAO’s World Food Day (October 16, 2023) with the theme: "Water is Life, Water is Food. Leave No One Behind." This theme aimed to spotlight water as the fundamental source of life, sustenance, and food”. From 26 to 30 October, 2023, our team of 25 dedicated volunteers (youths) visited four schools, engaging over 200 pupils and 30 teachers. Using communication tools provided by the FAO, we effectively conveyed the significance of water in sustaining life and food through four educative videos (Water is life, water is food, Water heroes are #FoodHeroes, Eight actions we can all take to save water, WFD official music video), hands-on outdoor gardening, water filtration experiment, WFD poster contest explanation, singing and dancing. The children had fun all he way

Schools Visited

1. Kings and Queens Primary School, Ashi, Ibadan - 46 pupils and 10 teachers
2. Bumble Bee Elementary School, new Bodija, Ibadan - 50 pupils and 7 teachers
3. VASTAB Mega Primary School, old Bodija Ibadan - 75 pupils and 10 teachers
4. Tekhols Primary School, Bashorun Ibadan - 40 pupils and 7 teachers

By fostering awareness and encouraging a sense of responsibility towards water resources, we strive to contribute to a sustainable and food-secure future.
